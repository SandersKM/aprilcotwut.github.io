My name is April Walker and I am currently a graduate student at the University of Arkansas. I'm a second year in Statistics and Analytics program and aspiring Data Scientist.

My current research attempts to determine if urbanization is impacting the intensity and frequency of heat waves. I do this using extreme value theory, specifically by taking the absolute maximum temperature for a given season each year for some frame of time and fitting that data to the Generalized Extreme Value distribution using Bayesian Inference.
